What is ACS(Astar Contribution Score)?

    ACS(Astar Contribution Score) is a campaign to incentivize activities and liquidity on Soneium by rewarding 100M ASTR to participants, expanding ASTR's utility as a core asset on Soneium.
    
Campaign Period: Feb 20th ~ May 30th, running for 100 days
Total 1 Billion ACS
1 ACS = 0.1 ASTR Airdrop
Total ACS Allocation: TVL-Based Allocation (70%) / Discretionary Allocation (26.5%) / Astar Surge (2.5%) / Soneium Meme Spark Contest (0.5%) / Campaign Reserve (0.5%)

How to get ACS on Untitled Bank?
TVL-Based Allocation
All the supply actions (including supplying for collateral) are eligible
Supply Bank
UB-USDC / UB-WETH / UB-ASTR / Neemo-ASTR-Bank
Supply Collateral (Total 7 markets)
ASTR Collateral: ASTR/USDC, ASTR/WETH
USDC Collateral: USDC/ASTR, USDC/WETH
WETH Collateral: WETH/USDC, WETH/ASTR
nsASTR Collateral: nsASTR/ASTR

    
Discretionary Allocation
100%: Borrow Assets (Equally split across 7 borrowing markets)
but, the weight values for each market can change every 10-day phase based on factors such as market-specific demand or the addition of new markets
Untitled Bank Season 1(first 10 days) Allocation: 2,500,000 ACS
Daily ACS allocation for each market: 250,000 / 7 = 35,714 ACS

    
Astar Surge
All the bridged ASTR to Soneium will count toward ACS
Astar Surge retroactive points will be allocated in the beginning of Season 5 (after 40 days)

  🚨 Major UI Updat for ACS: All the banks and markets are showing ‘Net APY’
    
Net APY = Native APY + ACS Supply APY + ACS Borrow APY
The Borrow APY, which was originally expressed as a positive number, has been changed to be displayed as a negative number since it represents interest that needs to be paid.
ex.   -3.00% APY means you are paying 3.00% interest.
